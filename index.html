OUR PROCESS

How We Treat Wastewater

Our comprehensive treatment process ensures the highest quality results while being environmentally responsible.

Preliminary Treatment

Removal of large solids and grit through screening and sedimentation.

Primary Treatment

Settling tanks remove organic and inorganic solids by gravity.

Secondary Treatment

Biological processes break down organic matter using microorganisms.

Tertiary Treatment

Advanced filtration and disinfection for water reuse applications.

Sludge Treatment

Processing and disposal of residual solids in an eco-friendly manner.
